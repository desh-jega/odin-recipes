Lasagna

Description

Lasagne are a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets. Either term can also refer to an Italian dish made of stacked layers of lasagne alternating with fillings

Ingredients

  • Red Wine
  • Beef Mince
  • Fresh Lasagne sheets
  • Tomato Paste

Steps

  1. First, spread an even layer of bolognese sauce over the base of an oven-proof dish
  2. Then, place a single layer of pasta sheets on top
  3. Next, spread over a layer of the white sauce (or b√©chamel) and repeat the process until both sauces are used up
